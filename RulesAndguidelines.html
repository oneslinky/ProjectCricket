Rules and Guidelines

1. Character Creation

  • Characters must be original creations or properly licensed characters from existing media.
  • All character stats and abilities must be balanced to ensure fair gameplay.
  • Players must provide a brief backstory for their character, including name, age, and background.

2. Gameplay Mechanics

  • The game will utilize a turn-based combat system, where players take turns executing actions.
  • Each character will have a set of abilities with cooldowns and resource costs.
  • Health Points (HP) and Mana Points (MP) will be tracked throughout the battle.

3. Fair Play

  • Players must adhere to the game's rules and guidelines at all times.
  • Any form of cheating or exploiting game mechanics will result in disqualification.
  • Players are encouraged to report any suspicious behavior to the game moderators.

4. Community Guidelines

  • Players must treat each other with respect and courtesy.
  • No offensive language, harassment, or discrimination will be tolerated.
  • Players are encouraged to provide constructive feedback to help improve the game experience.

5. Updates and Changes

  • The game developers reserve the right to update the rules and guidelines as needed.
  • Players will be notified of any significant changes to the game mechanics or community guidelines.
  • Feedback from the player community will be considered when making updates to the game.
